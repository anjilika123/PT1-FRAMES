1. Sinigang

Ingredients:pork belly , young tamarind , spinach chopped, string beans, eggplants sliced, daikon radish, okra, tomatoes sliced, long green pepper, sliced onions , water, fish sauce, ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil the young tamarind in 2 quarts of water for 40 minutes. Filter the tamarind broth using a kitchen sieve or a strainer. Squeeze the tamarind afterwards to extract its remaining juices.
  2. Pour the tamarind broth into a cooking pot. Let it boil and then add the onion, pork belly, and half the amount of the tomatoes.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Don’t worry if the butter solidifies a little.
  4. Skim-off the floating scums, pour 1 tablespoon fish sauce, cover and continue to simmer for 1 hour.
  5. Add daikon radish and eggplants. Cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add the long green pepper, string beans, remaining tomatoes, and okra. Cook for 3 minutes.
  7. Add the chopped water spinach stalks and season with fish sauce and ground black pepper. Cook for 2 minutes.
  8. Put the water spinach leaves. Cover and turn the heat off. Let the residual heat cook the leaves for 3 minutes before serving.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
