1. Menudo

Ingredients:pork, pig liver, cup potatoes diced, cubed carrots, soy sauce, lemon, chopped onion, minced garlic, sugar, tomato sauce, water, hotdogs sliced diagonally, cooking oil, dried bay leaves, salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Combine the diced pork and pig liver in a bowl with soy sauce and juice from the lemon.
  2. Season with pepper and marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat cooking oil in a pot.
  4. Saut√© the minced garlic and chopped onion until fragrant.
  5. Drain the marinated meat and brown it in the pot.
  6. Pour in the tomato sauce, the reserved marinade, and water.
  7. Add the dried bay leaves, sugar, and salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Bring to a boil, then cover and simmer until the pork is tender.
  9. Stir in the diced potatoes and cubed carrots.
  10. Continue to simmer until the vegetables are cooked through.
  11. Add the hotdogs sliced diagonally and let simmer for 5 minutes.
  12. Adjust the seasoning before serving hot.
